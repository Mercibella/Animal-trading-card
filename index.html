Giraffe

Long, black tongue can grow to 18inches long.

  • Scientific Name: Giraffa Camelopardis
  • Average Height: 4m-6m(13ft-20ft)
  • Average Lifespan: 20-25years
  • Habitat: Open woodland and savannah

The giraffe is a tall, long-necked herbivorous mammal native to Africa. It is the world's tallest living terrestrial animal, known for its distinctive spotted coat and long neck.
